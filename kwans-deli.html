ü•ò About Kwan's Deli

Family-owned Korean-American fusion deli located on the ground level of Embassy Suites. Operating since 2003, Kwan's offers both traditional deli fare (sandwiches, salads, burgers) and authentic Korean cuisine (bulgogi, bibimbap, katsu, Korean fried chicken). Known for generous portions and fresh ingredients. Popular with both hotel guests and downtown workers.

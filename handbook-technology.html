Electronic Resources

The Company provides electronic resources including computers, email systems, internet access, and software for business purposes. These resources are Company property and should be used primarily for work-related activities.

Monitoring: The Company has the right to monitor the use of its information technology and any electronic communications. Team members should have no expectation of privacy when using Company electronic resources.

Software Code of Ethics

Team Member Responsibility:

  • Team members may not copy software for personal use
  • Team members may not give software to any outsiders including contractors or customers
  • Software may only be used on multiple machines in accordance with license agreements
  • Team members may not download software from the internet and install it on company computers without authorization

The Company reserves the right to audit any company computer to determine what software is installed on the local drive(s).

Computer and Systems Security

All team members are responsible for the security of Company computer systems and data. This includes:

  • Protecting passwords and not sharing login credentials
  • Logging out of systems when leaving workstations
  • Reporting any security concerns or suspicious activity immediately
  • Not installing unauthorized software or hardware
  • Following all IT security policies and procedures

Email Content Screening

The Company reserves the right to monitor and review email communications sent and received using Company email systems. Email should be used for business purposes and team members should maintain professionalism in all electronic communications.

Use of Company Equipment and Resources

Company equipment including computers, phones, copiers, and other technology should be used primarily for business purposes. Limited personal use may be permitted but should not interfere with work responsibilities.

Team members are responsible for the proper care and security of Company equipment assigned to them.

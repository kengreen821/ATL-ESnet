Guest Service Tip: Embassy Suites Centennial Park is located in the heart of Atlanta's entertainment district. All attractions listed below are within a 5-10 minute walk, making us the perfect home base for exploring downtown Atlanta. Many attractions offer package deals with our hotel.
Adjacent to Hotel
Georgia Aquarium Adjacent
World's Largest Aquarium
Address: 225 Baker St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 581-4000
Hours: Open 365 days/year, typically 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM (hours vary by season)
Distance: Adjacent - Less than 2-minute walk
Guest Rate: Anytime Tickets: $57/person | Under 2 years: FREE
Home to over 100,000 sea creatures including whale sharks, beluga whales, manta rays, and bottlenose dolphins. Features immersive galleries including Ocean Voyager, Cold Water Quest, Tropical Diver, and the new SHARKS! Predators of the Deep exhibit. Includes dolphin and sea lion presentations (first-come, first-served seating). Average visit time: 3-4 hours. Embassy Suites is a preferred hotel partner with exclusive package options.
Exclusive Embassy Suites Guest Rate: Our guests can purchase Anytime Tickets for only $57 per person (children under 2 are free) - a special discounted rate available only through Embassy Suites. General admission prices are typically higher. Ask our concierge or front desk for tickets!
Centennial Olympic Park Adjacent
Historic 22-Acre Urban Park
Address: 265 Park Ave W NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Hours: Daily 7:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Distance: Adjacent - Directly across from hotel
Admission: FREE
Legacy of the 1996 Summer Olympic Games and the crown jewel of Atlanta's downtown entertainment district. Features the iconic Fountain of Rings (world's largest interactive fountain incorporating Olympic Rings), playgrounds, gardens, and expansive lawns. Hosts large annual festivals including Independence Day celebrations and Juneteenth Atlanta Parade. Free audio tour available via mobile app highlighting Olympic history and monuments. Perfect for picnics, family photos, and relaxation.
5-Minute Walk
World of Coca-Cola 5-min walk
Interactive Coca-Cola Museum
Address: 121 Baker St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 676-5151
Hours: Generally 10:00 AM - 7:00 PM (weekdays), varies weekends - check website
Distance: 0.3 miles - 5-minute walk
Multimedia attraction showcasing the history of The Coca-Cola Company. Features the Vault of the Secret Formula, art galleries with memorabilia, 4D theater experience, and the famous "Taste It!" room where visitors can sample over 100 beverages from around the world. Meet the Coca-Cola Polar Bear for photos. Average visit time: 2-3 hours. Often combined with Georgia Aquarium visits via package deals.
Money Saver: Atlanta CityPASS includes both World of Coca-Cola and Georgia Aquarium with significant savings.
Chick-fil-A College Football Hall of Fame 5-min walk
Interactive Sports Museum
Address: 250 Marietta St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 880-4800
Hours: Daily 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM (last entry 30 min before closing)
Distance: 5-minute walk from hotel
95,000 square feet with over 50 interactive exhibits celebrating college football. Features 3-story helmet wall with helmets from 765+ schools, indoor 47-yard playing field, AI-powered GameON! experience, VIP guided tours available (11 AM, 1 PM, 3 PM daily). Kick field goals, throw passes, record yourself calling legendary plays. Chick-fil-A restaurant on-site. Named one of top 10 things to do in Atlanta. Average visit: 2-3 hours.
State Farm Arena 5-min walk
Sports & Entertainment Venue
Address: 1 State Farm Dr, Atlanta, GA 30303
Distance: 5-minute walk from hotel
Home of the NBA's Atlanta Hawks. Hosts major concerts, sporting events, and entertainment shows year-round. Modern facilities with prime downtown location. Check event calendar for current schedule of games and performances.
National Center for Civil and Human Rights 5-min walk
Cultural & Historical Museum
Address: 100 Ivan Allen Jr Blvd, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (678) 999-8990
Hours: Tue-Fri/Sun 12:00 PM - 5:00 PM, Sat 10:00 AM - 5:00 PM, Closed Monday
Distance: 5-minute walk from hotel
Powerful museum connecting the American Civil Rights Movement to today's global human rights movements. Features immersive exhibits including the emotionally resonant lunch counter sit-in experience, Freedom Riders bus, and the Morehouse College Martin Luther King Jr. Collection (rotating display of Dr. King's personal papers and artifacts). Recently expanded with new galleries. Average visit: 90-120 minutes. Included in Atlanta CityPASS.
Important Note: Deeply moving exhibits - tissue boxes available. Allow ample time to absorb the experience.
SkyView Atlanta 5-min walk
Giant Ferris Wheel
Address: 168 Luckie St NW, Atlanta, GA 30303
Phone: (678) 949-9023
Hours: Mon-Thu 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM, Fri-Sat 10:00 AM - 11:00 PM, Sun 10:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Distance: At Centennial Olympic Park - 5-minute walk
200-foot-tall observation wheel with 42 climate-controlled, private gondolas offering breathtaking panoramic views of downtown Atlanta. Each ride lasts 7-12 minutes (3-4 full rotations). VIP gondola option available with Ferrari-style leather seats, glass floor, and extended 20-minute ride time. Perfect for date nights, proposals, or family photos. Don't miss the nighttime light show! On-site refreshment stand (Energy Eatz) serves snacks and beverages.
Children's Museum of Atlanta 5-min walk
Interactive Children's Museum
Address: 275 Centennial Olympic Park Dr NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 659-5437
Hours: Mon/Tue/Thu/Fri 9:30 AM - 4:30 PM, Sat/Sun 9:30 AM - 4:30 PM, Closed Wednesday
Distance: At Centennial Olympic Park - 5-minute walk
Atlanta's only children's museum for hands-on learning through play, ideal for children ages 0-8. Features interactive exhibits including farm area, grocery store play, construction zone, climbing structure, art room, and STEM science bar. Daily programs and activities with the Imaginators (museum's dedicated educators/actors). Advance online ticket purchase highly recommended. Average visit: 1-2 hours. Perfect for families with young children.
10-Minute Walk or Less
Mercedes-Benz Stadium 10-min walk
World-Class Sports & Entertainment Venue
Address: 1 AMB Dr NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Distance: Short walk from hotel
State-of-the-art venue with retractable roof, home to NFL's Atlanta Falcons and MLS's Atlanta United FC. Hosts major concerts, college football championships (SEC Championship, Chick-fil-A Peach Bowl), Monster Jam, and other premier events. Features innovative design and exceptional guest amenities. Check event calendar for current schedule.

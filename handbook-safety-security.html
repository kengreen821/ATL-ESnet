Cameras and Video Surveillance

For the purposes of workplace safety and security and to prevent theft and other misconduct, the Company has installed video surveillance cameras in work areas.

If there is any reported incident of theft, trespass, workplace violence, team member misconduct or any type of safety violation, the Company will utilize its surveillance equipment as an investigatory tool.

Privacy: The Company respects the privacy of its team members. No video cameras will be installed in the Company's restrooms or in any lactation or changing areas.

Any unauthorized use of these video cameras and/or video recordings is strictly forbidden and may result in discipline, up to and including termination of employment.

Cell Phone Use / Texting While Driving

Team members whose job responsibilities include regular or occasional driving are expected to put safety first. Personal and company-supplied cell phones are not to be used while driving.

If a team member receives a call while driving, they must pull over safely, park, and then either answer the phone or return the call. Team members also may not send or review text messages while driving.

Team members who are charged with traffic violations, or cause accidents or injuries, resulting from their use of cell phones while driving will be solely responsible for all liabilities, fines, etc., that result.

Company's Right to Search

The Company wishes to maintain a work environment that is free of illegal drugs, alcohol, firearms, explosives, or other improper materials.

Desks, lockers, and other storage devices are provided for the convenience of team members but remain the sole property of the Company. They, as well as any articles found within them, can be inspected by any agent or representative of the Company at any time, either with or without prior notice.

We reserve the right to question and inspect or search any team member or other individual entering or leaving company premises. The inspection or search may include any packages or items that the individual may be carrying.

Driving for Company Business

All team members are expected to comply with all local, state, and federal laws while driving a vehicle for business purposes.

  • Team members required to drive must have and maintain a valid driver's license
  • Wear seat belts and travel at a safe speed
  • Team members using personal vehicles for business must carry insurance
  • Any accidents in company vehicles must be reported immediately to police and Human Resources
  • Vehicle engine must be shut off, ignition keys removed, and doors locked when vehicle is left unattended

Emergency Evacuation

In the event of a fire or hazardous material emergency, the emergency fire alarm system should be activated by pulling one of the fire alarms.

Evacuation Procedures:

  • All team members and visitors must evacuate the building immediately
  • Do not use elevators - descend stairwells in an orderly manner
  • Report to the designated meeting location away from building exits
  • Report to the safety coordinator or supervisor and remain until accounted for
  • No reentry permitted until official all-clear notification is given

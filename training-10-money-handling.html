Money Handling

Training Outline for Skills Training

Training Topic: Money Handling

  • The Bank must be counted in and out for each shift by an agent. While counting, the agent must have a witness. The bank should be 400$ when the shift changes.
  • One agent will "own" the bank per shift. This agent will be responsible for the bank key and count.
  • If cash is taken, the amount must be dropped at the end of the shift with a witness.
  • Agents need to close out their cash balancing and cash accounts before leaving their shift. This is a must!!

If you have any questions, please notify your MOD, and we will work with you.

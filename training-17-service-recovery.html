Training Topic #17

Service Recovery

Service Recovery Guidelines

1. Understanding Revenue vs. Non-Revenue Recovery

Revenue Service Recovery:

  • This occurs when we adjust a dollar amount from the guest's bill due to an inconvenience.
  • Example: Comping parking, removing a partial or full night charge.

Non-Revenue Service Recovery:

  • This involves offering the guest a solution to rectify the issue without removing funds from their bill.
  • Example: Room move, awarding points, BMG certificate, providing a snack bag, etc.

2. Steps for Performing Revenue Service Recovery

  1. Open the guest's folio.
  2. Highlight the charge requiring you wish to refund.
  3. Select the service recovery button (denoted by the $ symbol).
  4. Fill in the required information:
    • Amount
    • Category
    • Reason
    • Problem description
  5. Press "OK" to submit the request and adjust the amount on the bill.

3. Steps for Performing Non-Revenue Service Recovery

  1. Open the guest's reservation.
  2. Select the non-revenue service recovery button (denoted by the Pineapple symbol).
  3. Fill in the required information:
    • Category
    • Reason
    • Description (please specify the service recovery provided).

Service Recovery Compensation Levels

Always notify the MOD of any service recoveries, because they are required to track them on the MOD reports and on the tracker.

Use discretion and have compensation match the level of severity for the issue. Consult with MOD, if in doubt.

Management Approved compensation- No Exceptions

Small/Low-level compensation: (up to $55) a beverage voucher, 1 level suite move/popcorn, 1 level suite upgrade, Hilton Honors points 1000-5000, and an amenity bag from C&M.

Medium: (up to $110) RC voucher, Hilton Honors points 5000-10000, 2-level suite upgrades, suite movie/popcorn, 1 level suite upgrade, Hilton Honors points 5000-10000, 2-level suite upgrades, comp parking 2 nights, discount on suite 20-40%, 1 plus nights of parking, 3 level bag from C&M, suite upgrades (presidential, gov, centennial), 1 or more comp nights, Gift certificates to our location, BMG certificate, bottle of wine or RC amenity fruit tray

High level: ($110+) RC voucher, Hilton Honors points 10K-20K, 3-level bag from C&M, suite upgrades (presidential, gov, centennial), 1 or more comp nights, Gift certificates to our location, BMG certificate, bottle of wine or RC amenity fruit tray

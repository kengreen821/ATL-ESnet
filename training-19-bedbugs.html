Training Topic #19

Bedbugs

How To Deal With Bedbugs

  1. When a guest calls about possibly having bedbugs, never admit or deny that they have bedbugs.
  2. Go to the room, get all the necessary information from the guest for the incident report, such as name, cellphone number, address, etc.
  3. Take a picture of the bedbug(s) or insects in the room. let you know if you do not contact Ecolab to come out and inspect the room. Note: The room will will contact Ecolab to come out and inspect the room. Note: The room can only be inspected after the guest is moved to another room if we have one the hotel.
  4. If you do not contact Ecolab to come out and inspect the room, only be inspected after the guest is moved to another room if we have one available. If the guest does not want to move, we will have to wait for them the hotel.
  5. Ask the guest if it is ok to move them to another room. If the guest does not want to move, we will have to wait for them to check out of the hotel before we can inspect the room and the supervisor to check out of the hotel before we can inspect the room and an incident report, and the supervisor and/or manager will get back with them in a minimum of 72 hours.
  6. Inform the guest that you will do an incident report, and the supervisor and/or manager will get back with them in a minimum of 72 hours.

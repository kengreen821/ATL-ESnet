For Hotel Staff - Guest Inquiries

When Guests Ask About Atlanta Magic Theater:

  1. Check current show schedule (posted at front desk or in system)
  2. Verify availability for requested show time
  3. Important: Process ticket purchase with credit/debit card only - CANNOT charge to room
  4. Provide venue location details (check with management for specific location)
  5. Inform guests to arrive 10-15 minutes early for seating

Payment Reminder: Atlanta Magic Theater tickets cannot be charged to guest rooms. Guests must pay with credit/debit card at time of booking.

Show Schedule: Varies by season. Always check current schedule before confirming with guests.

Group Bookings: Refer large groups (10+) to Sales & Events department.

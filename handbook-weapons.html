Weapons in the Workplace

Prohibited items and security procedures

Weapons Prohibition Policy

STRICT PROHIBITION: The Company strictly prohibits team members, or any other person providing services to the Company or located on the Company's premises, from possessing weapons of any kind in the workplace.

The workplace includes any property owned or leased by the Company or occupied by groups of company team members or people providing services to the Company.

This prohibition specifically includes guns, rifles, and firearms of any type, including those for which the holder has a legal permit.

Prohibited Weapons Include:

  • Guns, rifles, and firearms of any type (even with a legal permit)
  • Knives
  • Ammunition
  • Bombs
  • Bows and arrows
  • Clubs
  • Slingshots
  • Blackjacks
  • Metal knuckles
  • Any similar devices that by their design or intended use are capable of inflicting serious bodily injury or lethal force

Company Vehicles

Team members are not permitted to transport or store weapons in vehicles owned or leased by the Company and used by the team member for work purposes, unless the team member is required to transport or store a weapon as part of the team member's duties, and they have written permission from Human Resources.

This policy prohibits the possession of concealed weapons as well as weapons carried openly.

Consequences

Violation of this policy may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination of employment. The Company takes this policy very seriously and will enforce it strictly to ensure the safety and security of all team members and visitors.

Workplace Policies

Solicitation, tobacco-free workplace, and equipment use policies

No Solicitation / Distribution of Literature

The Company has established the following rules applicable to all team members and non-team members that govern solicitation, distribution of written material and access to Company property:

  • Team members may engage in solicitation activities only during nonworking times
  • No team member may engage in solicitation during their working time or during the working time of the team member at whom such activity is directed
  • Non-team members are not permitted to solicit or to distribute written material for any purpose on Company property
  • Off-duty team members are not permitted in work areas
Definition: "Working time" includes all time for which a team member is paid and/or is scheduled to be performing services for the Company; it does not include break periods, meal periods, or periods in which a team member is not scheduled to be performing work.

Tobacco-Free Workplace

The Company is committed to providing a healthy work environment for all team members. Smoking, including the use of e-cigarettes and vaping devices, is prohibited in all indoor work areas and company vehicles.

Designated smoking areas may be provided outdoors. Team members who smoke must do so only during authorized break periods and in designated areas.

Use of Company Equipment

Equipment, vehicles, tools, materials, and supplies provided by the Company are to be used for Company business only unless otherwise authorized by management.

  • Team members are expected to use Company equipment properly and responsibly
  • Report any damage, malfunction, or safety concerns immediately
  • Equipment should be maintained in good working condition
  • Personal use of Company equipment requires prior management approval

Use of Company Resources

Company resources including office supplies, copiers, printers, postage, and other materials are provided for business use. Limited personal use may be permitted but should be minimal and not interfere with work duties.

Team members who abuse Company resources or use them excessively for personal purposes may be subject to disciplinary action.

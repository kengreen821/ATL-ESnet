Legacy Ventures Restaurants

Nearby dining options owned and operated by Legacy Ventures Hotels - all within walking distance of Embassy Suites Centennial Park

Guest Recommendations: All five Legacy Ventures restaurants are located within a 5-minute walk from Embassy Suites Centennial Park. These are great options to recommend to guests looking for quality dining near the hotel.
Dos Bocas
Cajun & Tex-Mex
Address: 275 Baker St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 704-8078
Hours:
Monday - Thursday 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Friday 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM
Saturday 12:00 PM - 11:00 PM
Sunday 12:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Two-story restaurant with outdoor patio overlooking Centennial Olympic Park. Features authentic Louisiana Cajun and Texas Tex-Mex cuisine combining the best flavors from the 300-mile stretch between San Antonio and New Orleans. Menu includes enchiladas, tacos, po' boys, crawfish, shrimp étouffée, and more. Known for generous portions and affordable pricing.
STATS Brewpub
Sports Bar & Brewery
Address: 300 Marietta St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 885-1472
Hours:
Monday - Friday 4:00 PM - 10:00 PM
Saturday 11:00 AM - 11:00 PM
Sunday 11:00 AM - 10:00 PM
Atlanta's premier sports bar featuring 16,000 square feet with over 70 HD televisions and seven separate audio zones. Houses a 10-barrel brewery with award-winning craft beers including IPAs, sours, lagers, and seasonals. Unique feature: functioning beer taps built into tables. Offers gourmet American cuisine including burgers, wings, nachos, and the signature Dirty Bird chicken sandwich. Perfect for watching games - walking distance to Mercedes Benz Stadium and State Farm Arena.
Der Biergarten
German Beer Garden
Address: 300 Marietta St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 521-2728
Hours:
Monday - Tuesday CLOSED
Wednesday - Friday 4:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Saturday - Sunday 12:00 PM - 9:00 PM
Atlanta's authentic German beer garden featuring 7,000 square feet of indoor and outdoor seating. Partnered with German native Wolfgang Hartert to ensure authenticity. Features over a dozen German draft beers served in traditional half-liter or one-liter steins. Menu includes Wiener Schnitzel, Jäger Schnitzel, bratwurst, wursts, sauerbraten, strudels, pretzels, and traditional German sides. Bavarian-themed décor includes a German-made model railroad that circles the restaurant. Spacious outdoor elevated patio with string lights.
Max's Coal Oven Pizzeria
New York-Style Pizza
Address: 300 Marietta St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 974-2941
Hours:
Daily 11:00 AM - 9:00 PM
Authentic New York-style pizza from Georgia's only genuine coal-burning oven. The coal oven creates the unique taste and texture that has been a hallmark of NYC pizzerias for over a century - crispy on the outside, chewy on the inside. Features house-made mozzarella and secret recipe red sauce. Full menu includes classic salads, pastas, Italian sandwiches, signature coal oven wings, and freshly made desserts. Indoor and outdoor seating available. Perfect for takeout to enjoy at Centennial Olympic Park.
Twin Smokers BBQ
Regional BBQ
Address: 300 Marietta St NW, Atlanta, GA 30313
Phone: (404) 698-4707
Hours:
Monday - Saturday 11:00 AM - 8:00 PM
Sunday 11:00 AM - 6:00 PM
Regionally inspired BBQ featuring two signature smokers and a "wood library" with four different types of wood. Beef is smoked Texas-style over Mesquite and Post Oak; poultry and pork are smoked Southern-style over Hickory and White Oak. Menu combines favorite BBQ flavors from Texas, North Carolina, South Carolina, Kansas City, and Memphis with Georgia flair. Features high-quality slow-cooked barbecue, fresh sides, craft beer, and an award-winning bourbon selection.
Note: Early dining recommended; hours may vary if food runs out.

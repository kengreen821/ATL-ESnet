Training Topic #3

Check-Out Procedures

Objective

To ensure that all Front Office personnel are aware of the procedures involved when checking out a guest.

Summary

  1. Greet the guest with a smile
  2. Ask the guest how you can assist them, and locate their reservation by room number or last name
  3. Verify the guest information and length of stay
    • Verify how their stay was with us and thank the guest for their loyalty.
  4. Ask the guest how it was
    • If it was not good, ask them why and follow up on the make it right procedures.
  5. Verify guest charges with the guest.
    • Most guests will have only one folio, but some may have additional folios if so, open those and verify charges with the guest's reservation, remember that the if the second folio is from a third party or point's reservation, remember that the if the second folio is confidential, and it will be displayed on the top left.

Training Topic #15

Valet Parking Incidents

Objective

To ensure that all Front Office personnel are aware of the SOP to be followed when a guest complains about an incident in the valet.

Summary

  • Listen to the guest's whole story
  • Apologize for the inconvenience
  • Call for the Parking Manager/Supervisor and give the guest PPI the incident report
  • Hand the guest over to the Parking Manager and advise the guest that you will forward the report to the Parking Manager reach out to the Parking Manager, you will forward the report to the Parking Manager reach out to guest within 48 hrs
  • Have Security complete the incident report and a care guest to the Parking Manager and Security will forward the report to the Parking Manager reach out to guest.
  • Security will scan the report and send it as a drive, in the last name and the issue in PEEP and label the guest as a Care guest.
  • Note the issue in PEEP and scan the report and save into Security folder under valet parking incidents. The valet parking incidents. They will then create an incident report for the name and the investigation is completed.

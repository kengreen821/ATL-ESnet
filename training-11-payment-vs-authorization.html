Training Topic #11

Payment vs Authorization

Training Topic: No payments while the guest is still in-house

Introduction

It is crucial to avoid hard-charging guests while they are still in-house. Always request before their check-out date can lead to the authorization being released, potentially preventing the hotel from authorizing the card again.

Awareness

Hard-charging a guest before their check-out date can lead to the authorization being released, potentially preventing the hotel from authorizing the card again.

Hard Charging Definition

Posting a charge on the receipt. This is different from an authorization hold, which only reserves funds without completing the transaction.

Authorization Failures

If an authorization hold is released prematurely due to hard charging the card, attempt to reauthorize the card. Notify the MOD immediately and request an alternative payment method if necessary.
